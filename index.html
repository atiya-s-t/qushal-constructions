Who We Are

Engineering Trust,
Building Futures

M/s Qushal Constructions is a Dharwad-based firm of architects, engineers, and contractors with over 25 years of delivering quality construction across Karnataka. We handle every phase — from concept and planning permissions to structural execution and final handover.

Our registered office is at F-2, 1st Floor, Devaki Annex, Near K.C. Park Post Office, Haliyal Road, Dharwad. We are equipped to take on projects of any scale across all building categories.
